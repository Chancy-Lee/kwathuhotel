Our Story

A Place You'll
Always Call Home

Kwathu — meaning "ours" in Chichewa — was born from a simple belief: every guest deserves to feel truly at home.

Nestled in the heart of Lilongwe, Kwathu Hotel blends contemporary comfort with the rich warmth of Malawian culture. From the moment you step through our doors, you're welcomed not just as a guest, but as family. We take pride in our attentive service, beautifully appointed rooms, and a genuine passion for hospitality that has defined us for over a decade.
